Disc Information
Information about this CD-ROM.

Well Data Index
An index of well data on this disc. All data types for an individual well are accessed through this index. Data types provided include:

  • Well Core Photographs
  • Depths to Selected Stratigraphic Horizons
  • Geological and Drilling Reports
  • LAS-Format Core-Gamma Ray Data
  • LAS-Format Borehole Log Data
  • Permeability, Porosity, and Grain Density Data
